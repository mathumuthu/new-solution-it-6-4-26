OUR STORY

Pioneering IT Excellence Since 2015

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between complex technology and business growth, Codeio began as a small team of three passionate developers working from a garage in East London. Today, we've evolved into a global force of innovation with over 150 team members across 8 countries.

Our journey has been defined by relentless curiosity and an unwavering commitment to our clients' success. From startups to Fortune 500 companies, we've helped transform digital landscapes across industries including fintech, healthcare, e-commerce, and enterprise software.

We believe technology should empower, not complicate. Every solution we craft is designed with human-centric principles, ensuring seamless integration and tangible results that drive real business value.
